Game Rules

1. Each player takes turns to place their piece on the board.

2. A player can move their pieces after placing 3 pieces initially.

3. The player must select one of their pieces to move to an empty cell.

4. The first player to align three pieces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.

5. Each player has 3 minutes to make all their moves. If time runs out, the opponent wins.
